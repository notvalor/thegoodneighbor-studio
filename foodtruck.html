La Noche Tacos

A fictional late-night taco truck built to show how a simple site can share a menu, weekly schedule, and contact details without getting in the way.

All names and locations are placeholders. The layout and structure are ready for a real client.

About the truck

La Noche Tacos serves casual Mexican street food: tacos, burritos, and a few simple sides. The truck focuses on a short menu with fresh tortillas and consistent recipes.

What they serve

The menu stays small on purpose: a few core tacos, larger burritos, and house-made salsas. This keeps prep tight and wait times short during late-night rushes.

Specials rotate by week, but the standard options below stay on the menu year round.

Where they park

Most nights the truck is within walking distance of subway stops and bars, with a focus on repeat corners so regulars know where to look.

This schedule is a sample. A real client would swap in live locations and times.
