How this section works

The featured listing reads like a short, honest summary. It avoids filler and sticks to layout, light, and location.

  • Photo first, so the property feels real.
  • Address and price in a consistent format.
  • Beds, baths, and square footage near the top.
  • One paragraph that focuses on what it is like to live there.
