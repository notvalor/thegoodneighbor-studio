How John works with clients

  1. Step 1 – Short call. Get clear on needs, timing, and budget.
  2. Step 2 – Plan and tours. Map out options, walk through homes, and sort what fits and what does not.
  3. Step 3 – Offer and closing. Help with offers, negotiation, and the paperwork that follows.
  4. Step 4 – After closing. Stay available for referrals, vendors, and local questions.
